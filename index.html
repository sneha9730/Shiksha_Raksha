ABOUT US

Shiksha Raksha is a distinguished online non-profit platform dedicated to fostering awareness throughout India regarding student dropout rates and the strategic measures to mitigate them. Our mission is to conduct comprehensive analyses of the factors contributing to student dropouts and to provide actionable solutions for their effective prevention. Shiksha Raksha serves as an invaluable resource for students, educational institutions, local communities, and the nation as a whole, with the overarching goal of significantly diminishing the incidence of student attrition from the educational system.

Our analysis focuses on addressing the issue of school dropouts in India through data-driven research. We aim to provide valuable insights to empower educational institutions, policymakers, and communities to take informed actions and improve access to quality education. Our commitment to data transparency and ethical analysis is central to our mission of reducing dropout rates and enhancing educational outcomes for all students in the country.

This slogan emphasizes how education (Shiksha) provides support, and protection (Raksha) leads to prosperity and progress.

CORE COMMITMENTS

At Shiksha Raksha, we have strong beliefs, a clear mission, and a vision. These three things work together like a compass to guide us in our mission of making education better for students everywhere.

VISION

"Shiksha Raksha dreams of a world where every child, no matter what's going on around them, can easily go to a good school. This helps create a future where there are lots of chances for everyone and a fairer society."

MISSION

"Shiksha Raksha imagines a world where every child, no matter of what circumstances, can get excellent education without any problems. This will make a future with lots of chances for all and a fairer society."

VALUES

"At Shiksha Raksha, we believe in fairness, kindness, honesty, including everyone, working together, new ideas, responsibility, giving power, speaking up for others, and the community. These beliefs guide us in making sure everyone can get an education."
